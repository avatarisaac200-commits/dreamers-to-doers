**Ifeoluwa Adeola Oyewole** is a doctor in training at the **College of Medicine, University of Lagos**, where she is currently studying **Medicine and Surgery**. With a strong passion for both healthcare and leadership, she has actively contributed to numerous medical student organizations, including the **Nigerian Medical Students’ Association (NiMSA)** and the **Federation of African Medical Students Association (FAMSA)**. Over the years, she has held several key positions such as **Vice Regional Director of the Standing Committee on Capacity Building, National Director of the Auwal Shanono Foundation, Technical Lead on Optometry for Public Health, Regional Director on Public Health and Advocacy, and Program Coordinator for FAMSA**, among others.  



Beyond medicine, **Ifeoluwa is a skilled writer, public speaker, and impact-driven leader**. Her ability to communicate effectively has earned her recognition on various platforms. She won **2nd runner-up** in an essay competition on "Burnout Syndrome" by FiniXX in 2022 and emerged as the **winner** of an essay competition organized by YALF. She shares her insights on **health, medicine, and life** through her blog on WordPress. As a public speaker, she has been featured on several TV and radio programs, including discussions on \*\*Schizophrenia on The Lagoon Radio, Depression in Young People on BCOS TV, Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (CPR) on FreshFM Lagos, and Epilepsy on SolutionsFM.\*\*  



Ifeoluwa is also an active member and executive of \*\*The Cardiology Student Network\*\* and a member of the \*\*American College of Cardiology\*\*. Her commitment to global health advocacy is further demonstrated by her role as an \*\*ambassador for The Royal Society of Tropical Medicine and Hygiene\*\*.  



Driven by her passion for cardiovascular health, \*\*she founded The Cardio Health\*\*, a healthcare initiative dedicated to \*\*heart health education, intervention, awareness, and advocacy\*\*. Through this platform, she has provided vital resources, healthy lifestyle tips, and outreach programs, impacting \*\*over 5,000 individuals\*\* both locally and internationally.  



With over \*\*four years of leadership experience\*\*, Ifeoluwa has attended numerous programs and courses on \*\*leadership and people management\*\*,. Her exceptional contributions to healthcare, leadership, and advocacy have earned her multiple accolades, including \*\*the 25 Under 25 Award, Most Inspiring Young Person of the Year, Speaker of the Year, NiMSA SW Executive Award, Outstanding Award in Planning and Execution, and Most Influential Person Award\*\*, among others.  



An ambitious and versatile individual, Ifeoluwa strongly believes in \*\*hard work, dedication, and the ability to thrive in multiple fields\*\*. She continues to inspire and drive positive change in healthcare, leadership, and beyond.
