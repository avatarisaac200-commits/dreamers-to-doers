Damilola Mogaji is a passionate Programs & Ecosystem Manager with over seven years of experience designing, executing, and evaluating high-impact programs across the tech, startup, entrepreneurship, social impact, and innovation ecosystem in Africa.

He has worked with local and global organizations, leading initiatives that empower young entrepreneurs, catalyze innovation, and foster strong startup communities. His expertise spans program development, stakeholder engagement, event production, partnership building, and startup ecosystem growth.

His career highlights include:
✅ Scaling Hult Prize Nigeria from 50 to 110 campuses in just four months through strategic campus partnerships.
✅ Leading the Nigeria Young Founders Week, bringing together 2,000+ student entrepreneurs from 15 universities.
✅ Spearheading large-scale hackathons, startup pitch events, and training programs at Consonance and Spark Hub.
✅ Driving education innovation in Africa through his work at Noved Africa, impacting thousands of young Africans with skills training and entrepreneurship programs.
✅ Organizing the Calabar Regional Impact Summit, which attracted participation from students and young leaders across multiple schools in Africa.
✅ Designing and leading the execution of the Undergraduate Students Summit in 2022 and 2023, addressing graduate unemployment and attracting over 2,000 attendees across both editions.
